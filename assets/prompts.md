# Asset Prompts — Micromachines V3 Clone

Prompts for generating placeholder + final art via mcp-image (Imagen) or video via Veo.

---

## Cars (V1+, 8 models)

**Style brief:** Low-poly toy car, ~50 polys, chunky and stylized like a Hot Wheels die-cast at 1:64 scale, crisp primary colors (red, blue, yellow, green), no decals. Render top-down 3/4. Background: pure white. Goal: drop into a Godot 3D scene as a temporary mesh.

**Imagen prompt:**
```
Top-down 3/4 view of a low-poly stylized toy race car in the style of Micro Machines PS1, simple primary color (red), no decals, chunky proportions, ~50 polygons aesthetic, isolated on pure white background, soft top-down lighting, render at 512x512, no shadows.
```
Variants: red, blue, yellow, green, white, black, orange, purple.

## Track 01 — Breakfast Table

**Style brief:** Top-down photo-realistic breakfast table seen from directly above. Large cereal box, milk carton, toast slice, plate, knife, jam jar — all positioned to form a natural racing circuit. Wood grain table surface. Bright morning light.

**Imagen prompt:**
```
Top-down photo of a wooden breakfast table from a child's perspective, with a large cereal box, milk carton, slice of toast, plate, butter knife, jam jar arranged like obstacles on a race track, bright morning light, realistic textures, 4K, no people, isolated objects.
```
